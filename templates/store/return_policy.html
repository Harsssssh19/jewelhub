Returns & Refunds

Return Window

Jewelry items can be returned within 30 days of purchase. The item must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging.

Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be unused and unworn
  • All original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Item must not show signs of wear or damage
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of purchase
How to Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Log in to your account and navigate to your orders
  2. Select the item you wish to return and click "Return Item"
  3. Follow the return instructions and generate a return label
  4. Ship the item back to us within 7 days
Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. If you're returning an item due to your preference, you may be responsible for return shipping costs.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please contact our customer service team at contact us.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom-made or personalized jewelry items cannot be returned unless they are defective or damaged.

Last updated: 2025. Our return policy may change at any time.
