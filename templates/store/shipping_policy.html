Shipping & Delivery

Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days): Free for orders over ₹500
  • Express Shipping (2-3 Business Days): ₹150 flat rate
  • Overnight Shipping (1 Business Day): ₹300 flat rate
Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends and holidays will be processed on the next business day. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Delivery Address

We deliver to all locations within India. Please ensure you provide a complete and accurate delivery address to avoid delays or delivery issues.

Shipping Restrictions

Currently, we do not ship to international addresses. We only deliver within India.

Lost or Damaged Items

If your package arrives damaged or is lost, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will investigate the issue and provide a replacement or refund as applicable.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you can track it using the tracking number provided in your order confirmation email. You can also track your order from your account dashboard.

Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, please contact us or reach out to our customer service team.

Last updated: 2025. These policies are subject to change without notice.
